The cell nucleus is one of the most important or most important organelles of all, it has extremely complex structure present only in the eukaryotic cells being the main factor that differs from the prokaryotic cells.

The nucleus is in constant activity whether DNA multiplication and protein synthesis, the nucleus is formed mainly by: chromatin, which is composed of DNA molecules and to a certain extent the meiosis originates the chromosomes, the nucleoli formed by proteins like DNA and RNA , which give rise to the ribosomes, the cariolymph that fills the space of the cellular nucleus composed of water and protein.

Its main functions is to control the chemical reactions and functions that happens in the cell, and store their genetic information of each individual.
