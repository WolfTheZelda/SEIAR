Planet Earth is the third planet in our solar system. Existing life is one of several characteristics that differentiates planet Earth from other planets in our solar system. The earth's formation date is 4.5 billion years, when its glowing mass cooled, creating the first rocks. The core temperature can reach 5,000 ° C. The moon is the only natural satellite on planet earth.

Biosphere or "sphere of life" are the elements that favor life on earth. Scientists believe that the atmosphere of planet earth formed from gases emanating from volcanoes after the formation of the planet. The earth's gravity is 9,807 m / s².

Its atmosphere is composed of:

Nitrogen - 78%
Oxygen - 21%

There are also small traces of argon, carbon dioxide, and water.
