Planet dwarf the solar system, Pluto is the ninth largest and tenth most massive object observed orbiting the sun. Data collected showed that Pluto has several mountains, valleys, volcanoes, craters and plains. Pluto was once considered the ninth planet of the solar system. However, after several discussions it was classified as a "dwarf planet" and our solar system now has 8 official planets.

Pluto is not the only dwarf planet in our solar system, there are also Ceres and Eris. The gravity of pluto is 0.62 m / s². Analysis of its surface shows that the atmosphere is composed of 98% nitrogen ice and traces of methane and carbon monoxide.
